ABOUT WATTIN CONTRACTING COMPANY

EXCELLENCE, INTEGRITY, AND INNOVATION, EVERY STEP OF THE WAY.

WATTIN CONTRACTING COMPANY is a Saudi-based contracting firm specializing in electrical, MEP, civil, and low current works. Our experienced engineers, supervisors, and skilled workforce ensure quality execution, safety compliance, and timely delivery.

Our Vision

To become a leading contracting company in Saudi Arabia, recognized for quality workmanship, safety standards, and long-term client trust.

Our Mission

To deliver cost-effective and technically sound contracting solutions while maintaining the highest standards of health, safety, and quality.
